Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Introduction

Bachat Snag is designed to help users capture, scan, and manage visual information using on-device image recognition features. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app may access, how that data is used, how it is stored, and the choices you have.

This policy applies only to the Bachat Snag mobile application. It does not cover third-party websites or services that may be linked from the app.

Information we may collect

Information provided directly by you

  • The app does not require account creation or personal identity details to function.
  • If a future feature requests optional contact information, feedback, or support details, that data will be collected only when you explicitly provide it.
  • Personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or payment information is not collected by default.

Device permissions and access

Bachat Snag may request the following permissions in order to provide core functionality:

  • Camera access: Required to capture images, scan documents, or recognize text and objects in the camera view.
  • Biometric authentication: If the app supports device-level biometric authentication, fingerprint or face data is processed by the operating system and is not transmitted to or stored by the app.

The app uses these permissions only for the features you choose to activate. If you deny a permission, the related feature may not work.

Automatically collected technical data

The app may collect the following non-personal technical information automatically:

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Operating system version
  • App version and build information
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • General usage patterns, such as which screens are used and which app features are accessed

This data is used to improve compatibility, troubleshoot issues, and optimize performance. It is collected in a form that does not identify individual users.

Local data created by the app

  • Photos, scanned images, and recognized text are stored locally on your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share them.
  • The app does not automatically upload local image data or scan results to an external server unless a feature specifically states otherwise.

How we use information

We use the data described above for the following purposes:

  • To provide the app’s scanning, recognition, and camera-based features.
  • To maintain and improve app performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • To diagnose and fix issues, including crashes or errors.
  • To understand how the app is being used so we can prioritize future improvements.

Data storage and retention

  • Information created or stored by the app on your device remains on your device unless you choose to share or export it.
  • Temporary diagnostic data may be retained only as long as needed to resolve issues.
  • We do not retain user data on external servers for analytics or backup, except where required by a specific feature and with your consent.

Local processing

Bachat Snag is designed to perform image scanning and text recognition primarily on-device. Local processing helps keep your data private and reduces the need for network transmission.

If the app offers a feature that uses a remote service, that feature will clearly explain what data is sent, and you will be able to opt in before any upload occurs.

Third-party services and libraries

Bachat Snag may use third-party libraries, SDKs, or services for functionality such as:

  • Camera control and image capture
  • Text recognition and machine learning inference
  • Analytics, logging, or crash reporting
  • Dependency management

These third parties may collect limited technical information as part of their service. Any third-party SDK used by the app is restricted to the minimum permissions needed to perform its function.

Third-party services may have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing those policies if you want detailed information about their data practices.

Data sharing and disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to advertisers or third parties.

The app may share the following types of information only in limited cases:

  • Aggregated, anonymized usage data with service providers to help improve the app
  • Diagnostic and crash reports to help fix issues and improve stability
  • Data required to comply with legal obligations or to respond to lawful requests from government authorities

Any sharing of data is limited to the minimum necessary to provide or improve app services.

Security practices

We take reasonable measures to protect the data handled by the app, including:

  • Restricting access to sensitive functionality through device permissions
  • Storing app data locally rather than transmitting it unnecessarily
  • Using secure operating system APIs for camera and biometric access

No system can be completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute protection against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

User choices and controls

You can manage the app’s access and behavior through your device settings:

  • Camera permission: Enable or disable camera access in your device’s permission settings.
  • Biometric access: Manage biometric authentication through your device security settings.
  • App storage: Clear the app cache or app data from your device settings to remove locally stored content.

If the app adds optional account, backup, or cloud-sync features in the future, those features will include explicit controls for enabling or disabling data sharing.

Children and age restrictions

Bachat Snag is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected personal data from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements.

When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.

Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the app developer through the app store listing or project documentation.

For the most accurate response time and support, refer to the developer contact information provided alongside the app download.
